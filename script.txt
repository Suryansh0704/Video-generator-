I was at the coffee shop, just trying for my morning latte. A simple task, you’d think, but no. This person, with UNBELIEVABLE entitlement, cut the entire line, then loudly ordered a ridiculously complicated drink, oblivious to the queue.

The nerve, right? My patience was truly tested. My internal monologue was a string of expletives. They acted as if their time was more valuable than everyone else’s. The whole situation had me completely cooked. It was ASTOUNDING.

Then, the ultimate betrayal arrived. They paid with a crumpled twenty and demanded exact change. Who does that? This utter disregard made everyone wait even longer; it makes me wonder, just like how I was at the coffee shop, just trying for my morning latte.
