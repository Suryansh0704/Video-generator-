I was horrified by the impunity of ancient kings who built their vast CASTLES on the backs of enslaved people, considering lives utterly disposable. This dark fact, a historical scar, burns with rage within me, their audacity sickening.

Imagine souls toiling, literally cooked by relentless heat, families starving while the monarch adorned his opulent CROWN. Tales whispered of torturous DUNGEONS, where rebellion was swiftly and brutally silenced, maintaining a chilling aura of absolute power over everyone.

I once believed past atrocities were simply accepted, a grim part of history; but unearthed SKELETONS and silent screams from shallow COFFINS remind me such suffering can never be truly forgotten; their plea for justice continues, always horrifying me with the impunity of ancient kings.
